About

The Gifs API was created with the intention to create an awesome experience to the user. All the GIFS are provided by Giphy.com, which is the largest GIF library in the world, which includes millions of original GIFS to make the user experience even more excited! Gifs API also offers the Favorites Page for you to save the funniest GIFS ever! Have Fun!

Instructions

Search for any type of animal and press "Continue" to generate a new button on the website. Simply click on the new button to see all the Giphy related to your search. Double click on the gif to animate.

To delete the new button you just created or the buttons existent on the website (ex. Goat, Dog), just click twice on the top of button. Every time the page is refresh, all the buttons created will be deleted.

On the home page, if you would like to save a gif click on the outline heart. To see all your favorited gifs go to the Favorites Page (top right hand corner). The gifs are save on Local Storage.

The ratings on all the gifs are provide by Gyphy.com. Examples include Y, G, PG, PG-13 and R.

The Downlaod Icon has a special feature! If you are interested in download the gif on your computer, click on the download icon on your Home Page or Favorites Page. The download will automatically start after you click the icon.

To add 10 more gifs on your page, click to either the Arrow Icon or the button "Load More" on the bottom of your page. Every time you click, will automatically add 10 more gifs.
